Gadimes Cave

A little known destination for foreign tourists, the Marble Cave must be famous in the world. It is located near the Gadime village, which was discovered in 1969. Most of the cave is still unexplored. Up to now, 1500 meters of full space have been explored, with 800 meters open to visitors. Inside are an impressive show of beautiful formations. Stalagmites, stalactites and argon are cave characteristics. It costs only 3 euros to enter where this guide includes the tour. It is a beautiful cave and well worth a visit.
